About Us

My steel dealing business in Ghana started in the early 2000s when I saw a growing demand for steel products in the mining industry. As the mining sector in Ghana expanded, there was a need for high-quality and durable steel for construction, transportation, and equipment used in mining operations.

I initially established contacts with local steel manufacturers and suppliers to source the best quality products at competitive prices. I built a reputation for reliability and prompt delivery, which helped me secure long-term contracts with major mining companies in the region.

Over the years, I expanded my product range to include a wide variety of steel products such as plates, rods, pipes, and fittings to meet the diverse needs of the mining industry. I also invested in storage and logistics infrastructure to ensure efficient distribution and delivery of steel products to mining sites across the country.

Through my dedication to customer satisfaction and consistent quality, my steel dealing business has become a trusted supplier to the mining industry in Ghana. I continue to adapt and innovate to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
